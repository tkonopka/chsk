# Chart Gallery

The gallery holds examples of complete charts built using components from `@chsk/core` and `@chsk` add-on packages.

## Datasets

All examples use synthetic datasets. ## Interactivity

Many of the examples in the gallery are interactive. For instance, some charts display tooltips upon mouse interactions,
or adjust state upon toggling items in legends. Other charts have buttons to download datasets or svg images.
However, it is important to stress that some charts are non-interactive by design.

## Milestone animations

Some examples are prepared with 'milestone animations' - a mechanism for step-by-step story-telling.

By default, the examples display the final state of the milestone animation. This makes it possible to grasp the overall form of all the charts while scrolling through the gallery.

To see the animations, click the 'first-page' button, <span class="icon"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" height="24" width="24"><path fill="#222222" d="M6 18V6h2v12Zm11 0-6-6 6-6 1.4 1.4-4.6 4.6 4.6 4.6Z" /></svg></span>, to reset the chart state. Then, click the 'next' button, <span class="icon"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" height="24" width="24"><path fill="#222222" d="M9.4 18 8 16.6l4.6-4.6L8 7.4 9.4 6l6 6Z" /></svg></span>, to step through the animation.

The gallery holds examples of complete charts built using components from the core library and from add-on packages.

## Interactivity

Most examples in the gallery are interactive. Features vary from individual charts, but may include:

-   **tooltips** that appear upon mouse hover;
-   **legends** that toggle chart state;
-   **toolbars** to zoom and pan;
-   **buttons** to download data or image files.

A small number of examples are non-interactive by design. These should be labeled.

(Note that gallery examples do not have storybook controls.)

## Milestone animations

Some examples are prepared with 'milestone animations' - a mechanism for step-by-step story-telling.

By default, the examples display the final state of the milestone animation.
This makes it possible to grasp the overall form of all the charts while scrolling through the gallery.

To see the animations, click the 'first-page' button, <span class="icon"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" height="24" width="24"><path fill="#222222" d="M6 18V6h2v12Zm11 0-6-6 6-6 1.4 1.4-4.6 4.6 4.6 4.6Z" /></svg></span>, to reset the chart state. Then, click the 'next' button, <span class="icon"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" height="24" width="24"><path fill="#222222" d="M9.4 18 8 16.6l4.6-4.6L8 7.4 9.4 6l6 6Z" /></svg></span>, to step through the animation.
